Order A Delectable Range Of Sweets, Snacks, Biscuits, Dry Fruits And More From The Comfort Of Your Home.

Gupta Sweets has its roots established in 1996 in the form of a small retail Sweet & Namkeen shop in Tirupur, Tamilnadu. Sweet is always a memorable one when someone needs to convey their happiness with others. Gupta Sweets is concocting various types of heritage Indian sweets by using traditional recipe in order to keep the fresh aroma in it. We offer wide range of regional special sweets and delicious savouries to make your time more memorable. Sweets will always occupy it’s own place during festivals and special functions. However there are lot of yummy and delicious sweets which is not limited to the festivals but favorable to be ordered at all occasions.
